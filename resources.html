Flintridge Forrest Neighborhood

With Mid Century architecture present throughout, this neighborhood is full of gems designers and craftsmen will love!

Oak Creek Park, nestled inside the neighborhood, provides a great place to bring the family together, or just go take the dog on a walk. Shopping and international food options are steps away and there always seems to be new restaurants popping up to try out!


There are three entrances / exits to the Flintridge Forrest Neighborhood. Two on Memorial Drive and one on Rockbridge Road.

Convenient Commute

Located only 25 minutes from downtown Atlanta, quick access to Memorial Drive, Highway 285, and Highway 20 make the downtown commute incredibly easy and quick.


Emory University Hospital and the Center for Disease Control (CDC) are only a 15 minute drive away in nearby Decatur.


Indian Creek MARTA Station (train) is only 5 minutes away and is a quick and reliable way to get to and from the Atlanta Airport in about 45 minutes. Unlike other cities, the MARTA train will take you directly inside of the airport - so you can make your flight!

Adventure Nearby

Stone Mountain Path is a great way to bike, skate, or walk to Stone Mountain Park where you can enjoy breathtaking views of the Atlanta Skyline and piedmont terrain. They also have performances and entertainment for kids that changes regularly.

In fact, Stone Mountain Path (10 minute bike ride from the Rays Road neighborhood entrance) stretches all the way from Stone Mountain Park to Downtown Atlanta for those who are looking for a more intense workout!


The artsy city of Pine Lake is a short walk away - and worth a visit! This unique "micropolitan" features a lake with a sandy beach you can swim in during the summer months, fishing and boating are also allowed on a separate section of the lake, and there are trails hugging the edge of Snapfinger Creek.
