Welcome Home

Our neighborhood is conveniently nestled East of Atlanta, and West of Stone Mountain.
We also have an active Civic Association with several volunteer committees that help make Flintridge Forrest a great place to live!

Learn More
New in town?

Welcome to the neighborhood! We hope you enjoy your time here and take advantage of all the great activities in the area!

Beautiful Homes

From Mid-Century Modern to American Tudor and everything in between, there is no shortage of architectural gems.

Close To Nature

13 acre Oak Creek Park is located in the neighborhood and is waiting for your next family BBQ! A playground, baseball diamond, outdoor grills, and tranquil creek are just around the corner.

Close To Everything

Convenience is an understatement. Walk to the gym, the grocery store, the pharmacy, or take your bike out to Stone Mountain Trail - it's all right here. 10 minute drive to Downtown Decatur / Emory / CDC and lovely Avondale Estates. 25 minute drive to Downtown Atlanta.
