Welcome Home

Our neighborhood is conveniently nestled East of Atlanta, and West of Stone Mountain.
We also have an active Civic Association with several volunteer committees that help make Flintridge Forrest a great place to live!

Learn More
New in town?

Welcome to the neighborhood! We hope you enjoy your time here and take advantage of all the great activities in the area!

Beautiful Homes

From Mid-Century Modern to American Tudor and everything in between, there is no shortage of architectural gems.

Close To Nature

13 acre Oak Creek Park is located in the neighborhood and is waiting for your next family BBQ! A playground, baseball diamond, outdoor grills, and tranquil creek are just around the corner.

Close To Everything

Convenience is an understatement. Walk to the gym, the grocery store, the pharmacy, or take your bike out to Stone Mountain Trail - it's all right here. 10 minute drive to Downtown Decatur / Emory / CDC and lovely Avondale Estates. 25 minute drive to Downtown Atlanta.

Hiking

Oak Creek Park (inside Flintridge Forrest neighborhood), Stone Mountain Trail (5 minute drive), and Pine Lake (5 minute drive) offer plenty of options for hiking close to home.

Baseball

Oak Creek Park and nearby St. Timothy United Methodist Church both have baseball diamonds, just bring your glove and ball!

Cook Out

Got the whole family together? Multiple charcoal grills and an oversized gazebo with picnic table are also located in Oak Creek Park!

Community

Flintridge Forrest Civic Association has many ways to help you get involved with your local community - visit the contact page for more information!

Gardening

The community garden offers an area to come together with your neighbors and share ideas, learn, and grow your own produce.

Lifestyle

Flintridge Forrest offers a laid back and serene atmosphere where friends and families can relax and enjoy being together.

Entertainment

With close proximity to Georgia State University (2 minute drive), and downtown Atlanta (25 minute drive) there is no shortage of culture, nightlife, and activities to keep you entertained!

Food

Some of the best international food in the world is walking distance away! Nam Dae Mun Farmer's Market, Madina (East African restaurant), Royal Caribbean Bakery, and Wings 101 are just a few local favorites to get you started.

Shopping

Memorial Bend Shopping District is walkable for all of your name brand clothing, groceries, gym, furniture, beauty, salon, and fast food needs.
